The Unplug Series

The Unplug Series was a digital wellness initiative created by author and illustrator Gemini Adams. The series explored the relationship between technology, social media, and mental health, encouraging individuals to examine their digital habits and find balance in an increasingly connected world.

📱 Core Themes: Digital wellness · Technology balance · Social media awareness · Mental health · Mindful technology use

The Facebook Diet

The Unplug Series gained recognition through "The Facebook Diet," a book that illustrated how social media platforms like Facebook can impact daily life, productivity, and emotional well-being. The book offered practical strategies for reducing social media consumption and reclaiming time for real-world connections.

Key Publications & Projects

  • The Facebook Diet: A guide to healthier social media habits
  • Taking Timeout from Technology Survey: Research on technology usage patterns
  • Digital Wellness Workshops: Educational sessions on mindful technology use
  • Online Resources: Tools and tips for disconnecting intentionally

Media Recognition

The Unplug Series and Gemini Adams' work were featured in major media outlets including HuffPost, The Guardian, and various lifestyle publications. The series resonated with readers seeking practical advice on managing technology overload and finding balance in the digital age.

The "Taking Timeout from Technology" Survey

One of the signature research initiatives of The Unplug Series was the "Taking Timeout from Technology" survey, which examined how people unplug from digital devices, the challenges they face, and the benefits of regular technology breaks. The findings highlighted the growing need for digital wellness resources.

Why Digital Wellness Matters

As technology becomes increasingly integrated into daily life, the ability to intentionally disconnect has become a valuable skill. The Unplug Series addressed:

  • Social media addiction and its effects on mental health
  • Strategies for reducing screen time without feeling isolated
  • The importance of real-world connections and experiences
  • Creating healthy boundaries with technology at work and home
  • Mindful technology use for children and families

Legacy

The Unplug Series contributed to early conversations about digital wellness before it became a mainstream topic. Gemini Adams' work helped pave the way for greater awareness of technology's impact on mental health and the importance of intentional disconnection.
